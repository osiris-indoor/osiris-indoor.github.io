About Osiris

Osiris is part of Ubica2 project and is being created by Bitmonlab, the R&D department at Zed Worldwide.

Ubica2 has been funded by the Spanish Ministerio de Industria, Energía y Turismo. Plan Nacional de Investigación Científica, desarrollo e Innovación Tecnológica 2013-2016.


Número de expediente: TSI-100105-2014-196

Osiris is an Open Source server for Indoor Maps. Zed provides advanced services on top of Osiris such as indoor location, indoor navigation and speech recognition. We provide the perfect set of technologies for smart cities. Find more about our solutions  in the  Smart Projects web site and contact us through its contact page.
